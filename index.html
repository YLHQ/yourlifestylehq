Owner and Founder

Terri-Anne was born in Perth Western Australia and moved to Sunshine Coast, Queensland when she was a small child. She has been apart of the community for over 30 years and has seen it grow and mould into a fabulous place to live, work and play. She is a compassionate and vibrant lifestyle ambassador and believes that everyone deserves to live life to the fullest. "Your lifestyle HQ" came about after Terri-Anne realised that people were time and lifestyle poor. Her passion and experience for helping people, and her love of getting jobs done, will benefit others get time back for the people and things they love. "Tomorrow is not guaranteed so l want to help others find balance in life, so they can enjoy it to the fullest with no stress and no limitations.".
